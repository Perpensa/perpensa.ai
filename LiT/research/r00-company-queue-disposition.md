## Item-by-item disposition

### C1 — Primary bench dataset behind the 16–19% recovery claim (P0)
**Requesting seats:** CRUCIBLE 3, DISSENT 3.
**What is needed:** the baseline process, feedstock, particle-size basis, and grade–recovery curves defining the 16–19% recovery figure, plus any independent third-party validation beyond the VT conceptual study.
**Status:** `NEEDS EXTERNAL RETRIEVAL: LiT internal metallurgical test reports / lab notebooks behind the 16–19% recovery figure, and any independent assay or third-party validation. Request directly from LiT in diligence; cross-check against any VT test data referenced in the sponsored-research deliverables.`
**Gate disposition (carried to Round 01):** the core technology Strength is **demoted from Strength to Assumption**, confidence **Moderate-High pending data**, flagged **High-risk / Low-confidence** until the dataset and an independent validation are produced. W3 (`research/r00-flotation-scaleup.md`) and W9 (`research/r00-operator-qualification-cycles.md`) independently show piloted recovery typically lands below bench recovery, so the figure must be treated as a bench-stage, unvalidated claim, not a delivered performance number.

### C2 — Derivation of the $229M / $2.29B per-operator NSR production-value bases (P1)
**Requesting seat:** SPOT 3.
**What is needed:** which operators, commodities, and output volumes generate the small ($229M) and major ($2.29B) per-operator production-value bases on which the royalty model rests.
**Status:** `NEEDS EXTERNAL RETRIEVAL: LiT internal revenue-model workbook showing the operator set, commodity mix, throughput, and price assumptions behind the $229M and $2.29B bases. Request from LiT in diligence.`
**Gate disposition (carried to Round 01):** the revenue base is flagged **Unsupported-as-derived**; the per-operator value bases enter Round 01 only as illustrative, and Round 04 widens the sensitivity band around them. W4 (`research/r00-licensing-norms.md`) already establishes that a 1% whole-mine NSR is off-market versus a product-of-licensed-process basis, so both the rate and the base it multiplies are contested.

### C3 — Executed VTIP license, LiT filing receipts, IIT (ISM) Dhanbad agreement and IP terms (P0)
**Requesting seats:** CLAIM 4, DISSENT 3.
**What is needed:** any executed VTIP commercial/exclusive license, LiT patent filing receipts, and the IIT (ISM) Dhanbad agreement with its IP terms.
**Status:** `NEEDS EXTERNAL RETRIEVAL: executed VTIP license agreement (if any), LiT/VTIP patent filing and prosecution receipts, and the IIT (ISM) Dhanbad collaboration agreement with its IP-ownership and license terms. Request from LiT in diligence; the executed license, if it exists, is the single document that converts the IP claim from contested to supported.`
**Material finding from the locked base (Cited).** The locked `research/LiT_VT_Sponsored_Research_Agreement_2024.md` is a **sponsored-research agreement, not an executed license**. Under it: VT owns all Virginia Tech Project IP (Art. 13.1); LiT (Sponsor) holds only a **time-limited option** to elect a non-exclusive or exclusive license within six months of a written disclosure (Art. 13.4), which then **expires if a separate license agreement is not signed within three months** of election (Art. 13.5); on non-election VT/VTIP is free to license third parties (Art. 13.6). LiT's named Background IP is "Extraction and Refinement of Lithium into Lithium Carbonate and Lithium Hydroxide Utilizing Flexible Feedstock and Non-Acidic Reduced-Temperature Leaching Methods" (Art. 13.9.a), used by VT royalty-free for internal research. This **confirms an option, not ownership or an executed license**, and so deepens rather than closes the C3 gap.
**Gate disposition (carried to Round 01):** the "globally patented platform" claim is flagged **High-risk / contested**; consistent with the DISSENT gate (a) ruling in W1, it enters Round 01 as a **Weakness/Threat-leaning item, not a Strength**. IP remediation (securing an executed, assignable, field-of-use license plus filed applications) is treated as a **precondition to proceed**, surfacing as a tranche condition in Round 04 and a Round 05 milestone.
