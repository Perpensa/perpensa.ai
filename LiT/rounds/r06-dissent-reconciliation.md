# Round 06 — Dissent Reconciliation

**Unit:** Round 06 step 3 (Moderator Playbook §Round 06, process point 3): *confirm every preserved dissent is represented in the relevant document; resolve what can be resolved; record what cannot.*
**Scope:** the formal Strategy dissent log **D1–D6**, the cross-cutting **DISSENT-T4 neutrality hazard**, the two standing **open gates (a) IP and (c) model-choice**, and every **per-document embedded DISSENT flag** in SWOT / STRATEGY / EXECUTION_PLAN.
**Method:** each dissent is traced to its representation in all three synthesis documents, given a reconciliation status, and split into *resolved* (settled by ruling or by structure) versus *standing* (carried as a live condition, trigger, or unretrieved-fact dependency that cannot be resolved on the locked evidence). No dissent is dropped for narrative tidiness (Playbook §6 false-consensus control).

A dissent is **represented** when the document carries its substance at full adverse strength, not when it is merely footnoted. SWOT is the upstream risk source (Round 02), so several dissents appear there as risk-register entries and contingencies rather than as a numbered "dissent"; that is correct placement, not absence.
