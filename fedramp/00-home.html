FedRAMP Wedge: The Complete Project

From market research to a stress-tested strategy, assembled as one navigable set.

This is a complete pass of the Perpensa method applied to one question: how to build the company that wins the Series A through Series C federal-SaaS compliance wedge under FedRAMP 20x. It runs from raw market and technical research, through a structured adversarial deliberation by a six-person committee, into a synthesized strategy and execution plan, and finally a red-team teardown and pre-mortem that attacks the strategy without mercy. Read it in order for the full arc, or jump to any piece below.
