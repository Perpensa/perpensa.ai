FedRAMP Wedge: Product & Platform

The three product requirement documents for the company, plus the Perpensa platform-fitness analysis.

This set turns the strategy into buildable products. It holds three PRDs tracing the company's arc, an MVP that proves the single-model consistency thesis with one design partner, a V1 that becomes the repeatable commercial platform, and a V2 that defends and expands once a well-capitalized incumbent enters the market, followed by an honest platform-fitness analysis of whether Perpensa's own architecture can serve as the substrate beneath this company.
