## Slide 3 — Our Solution

*Visual: Two stage cards side by side, each with a gold header bar and an icon (gears for Stage One, a lab flask for Stage Two). An arrow points from Stage One to Stage Two. Three navy badge buttons run along the bottom.*

**A two-stage, internationally patented platform that improves recovery from any ore type, and from tailings that would otherwise be discarded.**

### Stage One — Physical Separation

Proprietary physical separation process that is effective across the full particle size range, including the fine-particle fractions that conventional methods discard.

- Feedstock and target agnostic: lithium, REEs, tailings
- 16–19% recovery improvement over conventional methods
- Bench-validated across feedstock types
- Can be used in standalone site rehabilitation settings using modular installations, or integrate into existing processing infrastructure on site

### Stage Two — Chemical Extraction

Low-temperature, low harm leaching process that refines concentrated ore into battery-grade and refined mineral products with dramatically lower energy and no acidic waste.

- Operates well below 500°C — no extreme heat/grid-enabled power
- ~30% energy reduction vs. conventional methods
- ~20% faster processing time
- Zero acidic byproducts — cleaner, safer operation

**Badges:** PCT Patent Filed · Bench-Scale Validated · Academic Partnerships
