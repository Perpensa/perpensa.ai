## MECE test

Each master finding occupies exactly one quadrant. Items that read across two quadrants (bull/bear twins, presentation-vs-substance, strength-vs-gap on one record) are recorded once in their primary quadrant and cross-linked, never double-counted. The four pairings held to that rule:
- T-PRICE (Threat) / O-MARKET tightening-balance leg (Opportunity) — one price-indexed exposure, two faces, shared $110/kg benchmark, not netted.
- O-POLICY (Opportunity) / T-COMPETE government-capital leg + T-LIABILITY (Threat) — same policy/Good-Samaritan door read as tailwind and as exposure; transmission boundary recorded once in T-COMPETE.
- W-GRADE (Weakness) / T-GRADE (Threat) / O-MARKET recoverable-minerals leg — resource gap, heterogeneity, and category headroom are three readings of one feedstock record.
- S-CHANNEL / W-BASE and S-FUNDABLE / W-CAPITAL — precedented structure exists (strength) vs exact rate/seed inadequate (weakness), carried together.

No strength carries kill-switch standing. Two weaknesses (W-RIGHTS, W-UPLIFT) carry conditional kill-switch standing with a preserved CONTESTED-SPLIT.

## Risk register, consolidated (RR-1 .. RR-26)

All 26 lines opened across Round 02 cross-examination, mapped to source master finding. Clearance triggers where the risk can be retired; mitigation triggers where it is structural and can only be sized and hedged.

- **RR-1 (High)** — no executed exclusive sublicensable field-of-use VTIP license. Clear: signed VTIP exclusive naming the minerals field with sublicense rights. [W-RIGHTS]
- **RR-2 (High)** — prior VTIP→MRC minerals license may foreclose LiT's field of use. Clear: FTO opinion + MRC carve-out confirmed (U-2, U-3). [W-RIGHTS]
- **RR-3 (Elevated)** — sublicensable license, if obtained, is royalty-bearing to VTIP, a cost LiT's royalty must cover. Clear: executed license with VTIP royalty modeled into LiT economics. [W-RIGHTS]
- **RR-4 (High)** — recovery uplift undefined/unvalidated; entire revenue model downstream of it. Clear: feedstock-specific uplift in pp vs named baseline, validated in continuous pilot at/above the required threshold. [W-UPLIFT]
- **RR-5 (Elevated)** — base mismatch: sub-stream uplift charged against whole-mine-NSR base collapses the value exchange. Clear: pricing where royalty base and validated value share one base. Structurally cured by RR-6; open until executed and reconciled to RR-4. [W-UPLIFT, via W-BASE]
- **RR-6 (High)** — whole-mine NSR base unprecedented; will be re-based by counterparties, compressing income. Clear: model re-based on incremental recovered value, return and sensitivities restated, whole-mine NSR retained only as a labeled ceiling. [W-BASE]
- **RR-7 (High)** — royalty base fully price-indexed, full non-curable downside. Mitigate: model across trough/mid/peak band, headline at trough, $110/kg floor as downside benchmark, explore floor/collar/minimum-royalty terms. [T-PRICE]
- **RR-8 (Elevated)** — exposure asymmetric (downside full, upside capped by lithium swing-supply). Mitigate: cap the lithium leg, do not bank open-ended bull income, weight central case conservative. [T-PRICE]
- **RR-9 (Elevated)** — adversarial price-suppression tail. Mitigate: stress-test deliberate suppression against the offsetting allied-policy floor, weigh basket/site diversification. [T-PRICE]
- **RR-10 (High)** — under-resourced for a relationship-intensive results-dependent sale; integration role only Year 5. Mitigate: phased hiring bringing BD + technical-integration forward, sized to realized load, funded by adequate/staged raise. [W-TEAM]
- **RR-11 (Elevated)** — on-site operating headcount for a continuous circuit unquantified. Mitigate: define the on-site staffing/support model per deployment, quantify LiT support headcount (U-19). [W-TEAM]
- **RR-12 (High)** — headline 27x–34x return internally inconsistent (two-year timing error) and stacked. Mitigate: corrected, internally consistent model run probabilistically at conservative inputs; base case at de-stacked downside, optimistic stack labeled upside only. [W-MODEL]
- **RR-13 (Elevated)** — exit anchored optimistic; revenue-multiple framing holds only at margins a first-scale company may not reach. Mitigate: two-framing exit (licensing base ~8x–12x EBITDA; royalty re-rate only after a diversified producing portfolio). [W-MODEL]
- **RR-14 (High)** — $2.0M seed single-tranche/reserve-thin; collapses if any deployment capital becomes LiT's. Mitigate: staged raise with explicit second-tranche/bridge, IP and pilot line items sized to the real work, hard no-client-capex line. Coupled to RR-10. [W-CAPITAL]
- **RR-15 (Elevated)** — 75% India / 25% VT cost base unevidenced; no executed IIT-ISM Dhanbad agreement. Mitigate: execute or formally cost the India R&D engagement, reconcile modeled spend to executed instruments, cross-link export-control (T-EXPORT). [W-CAPITAL]
- **RR-16 (High)** — recovery claim has no characterized resource at any site; $229M/$2.29B figures have no per-site basis. Mitigate: per-site characterization-and-screening protocol ahead of any deal, no royalty priced until the impoundment is assayed, headline figures restated as per-site-assay-dependent. [W-GRADE]
- **RR-17 (Elevated)** — REE/multi-feedstock geological basis unevidenced (only a single VA lithium-concentrate program). Mitigate: REE/additional-feedstock characterization before the multi-feedstock value is sold (U-13). [W-GRADE]
- **RR-18 (Elevated)** — heterogeneity forces fresh per-site characterization and undercuts the feedstock-agnostic pitch. Mitigate (structural): grade-led site selection, price characterization into every deal, sell selective high-grading, use federal tailings data layer as screening input. [T-GRADE]
- **RR-19 (Elevated)** — CERCLA PRP exposure and uncertain product title if LiT operates at / takes possession from orphaned impoundments. Mitigate: per-site title/liability diligence, liability-allocation keeping title/remediation with operator/owner, product-title confirmation as deal precondition, liability gate before any LiT-operated site (U-13/U-14). [T-LIABILITY]
- **RR-20 (Moderate)** — Good-Samaritan eligibility for a profit-seeker untested, pathway capped (15 permits, 7-year sunset). Mitigate: environmental-counsel opinion before any Good-Samaritan-dependent site, no Good-Samaritan cover banked in base case (U-15). [T-LIABILITY]
- **RR-21 (Moderate; coupled RR-16/17)** — per-site sampling the W-GRADE cure requires is itself a CERCLA-disturbance trigger if done unilaterally. Mitigate: characterize/pilot through, or under an access-and-liability agreement with, the title holder. [T-LIABILITY ∩ W-GRADE]
- **RR-22 (Moderate)** — distinctive environmental claims stated ahead of evidence. Mitigate: restate as validation-gated, tied to the same pilot that retires W-UPLIFT, no ESG magnitude in deck/term sheet ahead of result. [WAT-W1; cross-link RR-4]
- **RR-23 (Moderate)** — cannot anchor the running-royalty rate to a published comparable (closest deals confidential). Clear: primary benchmarking dataset obtained, rate quoted as a benchmarked range on the re-based base, first signed license treated as the definitive comparable; until then no rate presented as market-validated (U-5..U-7). - **RR-24 (Elevated)** — conservative incumbency-defended sale runs multi-year; first revenue likely later than Month-25; runway least able to absorb the slip. Mitigate: base-case first-revenue date on documented conversion reality, active-sites-first sequencing, runway sized to the qualification cycle. [T-ADOPT; cross-link CAP-W2, W-CAPITAL, RR-10/11]

Mitigate: position as the licensed process inside producers' plants, value-share terms tied to validated fine-particle differentiation, treat competitors' funders as the channel. [T-COMPETE; cross-link DIS-W5, ROY-T2, S-SCIENCE, S-FUNDABLE]
- **RR-26 (Moderate)** — government capital/offtake concentrate on scale producers; trade-protection contingent on Section 232. Mitigate: access non-dilutive/offtake channels through qualifying operator partners rather than as a direct applicant, do not bank trade protection or direct federal pull in the base case (U-11). [T-COMPETE transmission boundary; cross-link O-POLICY, WAT-O3]
