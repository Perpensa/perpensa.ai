## What D-1 is

D-1 is the structural split on the two foundational questions (the right to license the
core IP, and the validated recovery uplift):
- Alternative view: treat both as strict pass/fail preconditions; make no commitment to
  the plan as framed until both are in hand; a confident, fully built program presented
  before they are settled risks reading as permission to proceed.
- Working view: treat both as gating conditions to clear in a funded, time-boxed,
  kill-dated first phase that returns unspent capital on failure.
Both agree on the sequence: nothing commits until both clear. The split is on language
and posture, not on order. It is preserved, never resolved.

A second, coupled element of D-1: the two kill-switches (rights, uplift) are independent
single points of failure and must never be netted or blended into one score.
