## Divergent discipline (binding for this unit)

Each seat's option contribution below was written sealed, independent, and blind to the others. No seat references another seat or another option. Overlap across seats is expected and is left to the compilation step; it is not reconciled inside the contributions. These are parallel proposals, not a conversation.

The closing section ("Moderator compilation") is bookkeeping only: it dedupes the union of blind proposals into a MECE candidate option set with IDs for Round 04 scoring. It adds no new strategic content and judges nothing; scoring, attack, and selection are Round 04 work.

## Carried-forward constraints every "proceed" option must honor

These are settled outputs of Rounds 00-02 and are inputs, not options:
- Two gating preconditions sit ahead of go-to-market, scale, and any headline return: **W-RIGHTS** (executed exclusive, sublicensable, field-of-use VTIP license clearing the prior MRC rights, plus FTO) and **W-UPLIFT** (feedstock-specific recovery uplift in percentage points vs a named baseline, validated in a continuous pilot at or above the threshold the royalty math needs, reconciled to a congruent base). Both carry conditional kill-switch standing with a preserved CONTESTED-SPLIT.
- Curable-weakness cures are available and assumed inside any proceed option: re-base the royalty to incremental recovered value (RR-6), restate the model internally-consistent and de-stacked (RR-12/RR-13), stage the raise with a hard no-client-capex line (RR-14), and run a per-site characterization protocol before any deal (RR-16).
- The positioning decision that contains the two largest external threats at once: be the licensed process inside a producer's plant, not a producer-substitute (OC-4). This contains T-LIABILITY and T-COMPETE.

Item IDs below are `SEAT-O#` for seat proposals and `OPT-#` for the compiled set, so Round 04 can cite them.

## CRUCIBLE — process science and technology

**CRU-O1. Proof-gated, stage-narrowed validation sequence as the spine of the company.**
Make the validated science, not the platform claim, the asset the company is built on. Sequence the work so that the continuous-pilot validation of the recovery uplift (RR-4) is the first and gating deliverable, run on the one feedstock with an executed bench basis (lithium spodumene concentrate), and do not assert the multi-feedstock or REE-chemical-extraction claim in any commercial document until each has its own validated continuous result. Sell the part of the science that is real today, the fine-particle and ultrafine separation capability the open literature shows incumbents leave on the table, as selective high-grading of specific streams rather than as a universal recovery platform. The technical roadmap (REE chemical extraction, additional feedstock families) is presented as future optionality with its own gates, not as present capability. **Optimizes:** retiring the single load-bearing technical dependency before spend compounds on it; protecting credibility with technically sophisticated counterparties. **Leans on:** S-SCIENCE (route class credible, separation science world-class). **Contains:** W-UPLIFT, W-GRADE, T-GRADE, and the prior-art/reproducibility-parity pressure (CRU-T1). **Confidence:** the science is Cited; the uplift quantum is the thing this sequence exists to prove.

**CRU-O2. License the validated process narrowly, not the whole flowsheet.**
Scope the licensable unit to the stage and feedstock that carry validated data, and let the chemical stage enter the offer only as it clears its own bench-then-pilot gates. This keeps the freedom-to-operate surface small while the VTIP rights question (W-RIGHTS) is being cleared, because the company is licensing a defined, characterized capability rather than an undemonstrated full-platform claim that maps onto art it may not own. **Optimizes:** shrinking the rights-and-FTO exposure to what LiT can actually defend. **Contains:** W-RIGHTS surface, CRU-W2 (foundational separation stage maps onto VTIP-owned art).

## SPOT — market, demand, and commodity-price macro

**SPO-O1. Grade-and-demand-led targeting with price-band discipline built into terms.**
Choose sites and baskets where the supply-demand balance is tightening hardest (spodumene structural deficit forming, NdPr in a second consecutive deficit year) and where the in-place grade is high enough to carry characterization cost, rather than pursuing a feedstock-agnostic universal pitch. Pair the targeting with price-band discipline in the deal itself: model every royalty across a trough/mid/peak band, present the base case at the trough, and write floor, collar, or minimum-royalty provisions into license terms so the company is not booking income at a single fragile price point. Cap the lithium leg's modeled upside to reflect fast swing-supply restarts. **Optimizes:** capturing the real category tailwind without betting the model on the price cycle. **Leans on:** O-MARKET (large under-exploited base, tightening balance). **Contains:** T-PRICE (full price-downside, asymmetric exposure, suppression tail) and T-GRADE.

## CLAIM — resource, feedstock, and characterization

**CLA-O1. Single-site, single-feedstock, characterization-gated beachhead.**
Prove the whole value chain on one impoundment before claiming a portfolio. Select one operating site with a characterized, assayed high-grade tailings body; run the per-site characterization protocol (RR-16) ahead of any pricing; and price no royalty until that specific impoundment is assayed and the recovered value on it is established. Restate the headline resource and production-value figures as per-site-assay-dependent, and drop the multi-feedstock and rare-earth value from the near-term offer until a second characterized feedstock exists. The beachhead deal becomes both the proof of the model and the first definitive comparable for pricing. **Optimizes:** replacing asserted resource value with one verified case; turning the per-site characterization burden into a paid, sequenced gate rather than a hidden cost. **Leans on:** S-SCIENCE, the precedented deal channel. **Contains:** W-GRADE, T-GRADE, and the base/rate-anchoring legs of W-BASE (the first signed license sets the comparable).

## ROYALTY — deal structure and licensing economics

**ROY-O1. Re-based, value-share licensor inside the producer's plant.**
Charge a royalty on the incremental recovered value LiT creates or on a discrete recovered stream, not on the whole-mine net smelter return, and keep the whole-mine figure only as a labeled ceiling. Anchor the running rate as a benchmarked range until a primary comparable dataset and the first signed license set the definitive rate. Position the offer as the licensed process inside the operator's existing plant, with value-share terms tied to LiT's validated fine-particle differentiation, and pair the running royalty with an upfront fee and minimum-royalty floor so first cash does not depend entirely on realized production. Model the VTIP up-royalty as a cost LiT's own royalty must carry. **Optimizes:** a base counterparties will actually sign and that survives diligence; first cash de-coupled from a single price point. **Leans on:** S-CHANNEL (precedented bankable structures available today). **Contains:** W-BASE (base and rate legs), part of T-PRICE (minimum-royalty floor), T-COMPETE (value-share-inside-the-plant positioning), and the VTIP-royalty cost leg of W-RIGHTS (RR-3).

## MILL — team, business development, and go-to-market delivery

**MIL-O1. Anchor-operator partnership, active-sites-first, with a team built to the realized load.**
Land one anchor operator at an active operating site as the first relationship, and let that single deal define and fund the sequence rather than chasing a broad pipeline the team cannot service. Bring business development and technical-integration capacity forward to the front of the plan instead of a Year-5 integration hire, and size hiring to the realized deal-and-delivery load, funded by an adequately staged raise. Sequence active operating tailings ahead of greenfield licensees, where the operator already has plant, permits, and material moving, so the qualification cycle runs against a counterparty equipped to evaluate it. **Optimizes:** matching the thin, relationship-intensive channel to a thin, focused team; making first revenue real rather than scheduled. **Leans on:** S-CHANNEL, productized pilot-scale delivery. **Contains:** W-TEAM, T-ADOPT (slow conservative incumbency-defended sale), and the integration-footprint leg (MIL-W2).

**MIL-O2. Lead with paid validation-and-characterization as the first product.**
Sell the per-site characterization and pilot-validation work as a paid engagement before any royalty license, so the company has an early-revenue product that also produces exactly the proof (uplift on real feedstock, assayed in-place value) the royalty model needs. The validation engagement de-risks the two preconditions on a customer's site and on a customer's budget, and converts to a royalty license only once the proof exists. **Optimizes:** early non-dilutive cash, customer-funded de-risking of W-UPLIFT and W-GRADE, and a warm path into the royalty deal. **Contains:** W-UPLIFT, W-GRADE, W-CAPITAL (early revenue), T-ADOPT (a lower-commitment first sale).

## CAPITAL — financial model, capital structure, and exit

**CAP-O1. Disciplined asset-light licensor with a staged raise and a two-stage exit narrative.**
Hold the asset-light, no-client-capex model as a hard line: never use LiT capital to fund operator deployments, and keep deployment capital on the operator's balance sheet. Rebuild the model internally consistent, run it probabilistically at conservative inputs, and present the base case at the de-stacked downside (re-based royalty, trough commodity basket, conservative exit multiple), carrying the optimistic stack only as a labeled upside. Stage the raise with an explicit second tranche or bridge tied to the validation gate, and size the IP and pilot line items to the real work. Replace the discredited headline return with a two-stage exit story: a near-term process-licensing multiple on EBITDA, with any royalty re-rating reserved until a diversified producing portfolio actually exists. **Optimizes:** a plan and a return that survive a sophisticated investor's diligence; capital sized so a first-revenue slip does not end the company. **Leans on:** S-FUNDABLE (category investable, two defined exits), O-EXIT. **Contains:** W-MODEL, W-CAPITAL, the runway-exposure leg of T-ADOPT, and CAP-T1 (exit-multiple).

## WATERSHED — regulatory, environmental liability, and governance

**WAT-O1. License-to-the-title-and-liability-holder operating model, active sites first.**
Adopt as the default operating model a license granted to the party that holds title to the tailings and the remediation liability, sequence active operating sites ahead of orphaned or abandoned impoundments, keep title and cleanup liability with the operator or owner, and confirm product title to the recovered material as a deal precondition. Run per-site sampling through, or under an access-and-liability agreement with, the title holder, because the sampling itself is a disturbance trigger at a contaminated site. Reserve any LiT-operated or LiT-possessed orphaned-site reprocessing only behind an explicit environmental-counsel liability and Good-Samaritan-eligibility opinion, and bank no Good-Samaritan cover in the base case. Restate the distinctive environmental claims (no acidic byproducts, favorable remediation economics) as validation-gated rather than asserted. **Optimizes:** collapsing the severity of the environmental and title exposure through a choice the company controls. **Contains:** T-LIABILITY (CERCLA PRP and product-title exposure), the disclosure leg WAT-W1, and the sampling-disturbance coupling to W-GRADE (RR-21).

## CHOKEPOINT — geopolitics, supply chain, and allied positioning

**CHO-O1. Allied-recovery positioning with federal pull reached through operator partners.**
Position LiT's licensees as the natural recipients of federal offtake, the published price floor, and Title III / defense-industrial-base support, so that LiT's process becomes the enabler of a federally favored domestic recovery unit rather than a direct applicant competing as a thin pre-commercial licensor. Reach non-dilutive capital and offtake channels through qualifying operator partners, not as LiT itself. Confirm that the full target basket remains on the critical-minerals list and retrieve the eligibility question for a pre-commercial licensor before banking any of this. Do not bank trade protection or direct federal pull in the base case. **Optimizes:** converting the policy tailwind into LiT-adjacent revenue without overstating its reach. **Leans on:** O-POLICY, S-POSITION. **Contains:** the transmission boundary in T-COMPETE (government capital concentrates on scale producers) and OC-2's open eligibility/basket questions.

**CHO-O2. Allied R&D base with an explicit export-control and cost-base plan.**
Run the offshore, allied R&D footprint deliberately, with the export-control and IP-control plan attached from the start, so the geography reads as a cost-and-capability advantage rather than an unmanaged technology-transfer exposure. Put the R&D engagement under an executed instrument and reconcile the modeled cost base to it, rather than asserting an asset-light cost structure that is not yet contracted. **Optimizes:** capturing the allied R&D cost-and-capability advantage while managing its tension. **Leans on:** O-ALLIED-RD. **Contains:** the IP-control tension (CHO-T2) and the unevidenced India cost base (CAP-W5).

## DISSENT — non-base-case and narrower-business options

**DIS-O1 (DIS-P1 standing position). Do-not-proceed-as-framed: a rights-and-validation-only vehicle with a hard abandonment fork.**
Do not commit to the business as currently framed, and do not spend on go-to-market, team build, scale, or any headline-return presentation, until both load-bearing preconditions are resolved. Structure the near term as a minimal-spend vehicle whose only purpose is to (a) obtain the executed exclusive, sublicensable, field-of-use VTIP license clearing the prior MRC rights, with a freedom-to-operate opinion, and (b) validate the recovery uplift on representative feedstock in a continuous pilot against a named baseline and a congruent base. Pre-commit the decision rule: if either precondition cannot be cleared on acceptable terms and economics within a defined budget and window, the company does not proceed and the capital is returned or redeployed. This is not a delay tactic; it is an explicit option to abandon priced into the plan, and it is the honest reading of two independent single points of failure that must not be netted against each other. **Optimizes:** not spending the seed building a go-to-market on top of two unresolved single points of failure. **Anchors:** W-RIGHTS and W-UPLIFT kill-switch standing, CONTESTED-SPLIT preserved.

**DIS-O2. Narrower business: out-license or assign the IP position rather than build an operating company.**
If the rights clear but the company is unwilling or unable to fund the team, capital, and multi-year qualification cycle the operating plan demands, the narrower move is to monetize the position itself: out-license or assign LiT's cleared rights to an existing leaching-technology licensor or to a strategic operator already equipped with the channel, team, and balance sheet, and take value as an upfront, a royalty-on-royalty, or an acquisition rather than as an operating business. This abandons the build-a-company premise but preserves the value of the science and the rights if they prove out. **Optimizes:** capturing the asset's value without carrying W-TEAM, W-CAPITAL, T-ADOPT, and the full qualification cycle. **Contains:** W-TEAM, W-CAPITAL, T-ADOPT, T-COMPETE.

**DIS-O3. Narrower business: a single-deal validation micro-vehicle, no platform.**
Run the company as a one-deal proposition: one anchor operator, one characterized high-grade site, one validated feedstock, one re-based royalty, deliberately not a platform. Prove or disprove the entire thesis on that single deal at minimum burn, and treat any expansion as a separate, separately-funded decision made only after the first deal pays. This narrows scope until the evidence that would justify a platform exists. **Optimizes:** testing the thesis at the smallest spend that can produce a real answer; refusing to fund optionality ahead of proof. **Contains:** W-GRADE, W-UPLIFT, W-CAPITAL, W-TEAM, T-ADOPT.

## Moderator compilation — MECE candidate option set (bookkeeping only)
