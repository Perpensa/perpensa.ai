# Round 00 — Research Request Register (consolidated, deduplicated, prioritized)

Status: COMPLETE (sequential step). This is the governing register for the Round 00 research gate. The Moderator executes commissioned items next, one or two web items per iteration, writing each cited result to `research/r00-<topic>.md`. Round 01 does not open until every P1 and P2 item is either fulfilled in `research/` or flagged NEEDS EXTERNAL RETRIEVAL with DISSENT's agreement to proceed.

## Method

The eighteen raw requests from `rounds/r00-reading-notes.md` (R-CRU-1..3, R-SPOT-1..2, R-CLAIM-1..2, R-ROY-1..2, R-MILL-1..2, R-CAP-1..2, R-WAT-1..3, R-DIS-1..2) were merged where they ask the same question, deduped against what `background/LiT_Market_Research_2026.md` already discharges, and ranked. Twelve consolidated items (CR-1..CR-12) result. Each carries a disposition:

- **Commission** — genuine gap, web-retrievable; the Moderator runs it.
- **Confirm-only** — largely answered in the dossier; one web pass to update or verify, low cost.
- **Partial / NEEDS EXTERNAL RETRIEVAL** — the company-internal or legal-opinion part cannot be retrieved with the tools available and will be flagged as such; the publicly retrievable part is still commissioned.

Priority bands: **P1** gate-critical (a deliverable's spine depends on it), **P2** important (materially shapes a quadrant or option), **P3** useful (sharpens a claim, not load-bearing).

### CR-2 — How mineral-processing and process-technology licenses are actually structured
**Priority: P1.** Merges R-ROY-1, R-ROY-2.
Questions: What basis do real process-technology and mineral-processing licenses use (upfront plus running royalty on throughput, recovered-metal, incremental value, or NSR)? Is there any precedent for a whole-mine NSR royalty granted for a partial-stream process improvement? Benchmark the economics of comparable catalyst/flotation licensing deals (Jetti at Capstone Pinto Valley; Jameson NovaCell licensed to Jord International).

Output: `research/r00-license-structures.md`.
