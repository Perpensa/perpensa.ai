# Round 00 — Research Request Register (Consolidated)

**Status:** CLOSED for intake; OPEN for execution (next unit)
**Prepared by:** Moderator
**Date:** 2026-06-29
**Inputs:** 18 raw requests from rounds/r00-reading-notes.md (C1-C3, S1-S3, CL1-CL3, R1-R3, M1-M3, CA1-CA3, W1-W3, D1-D3)

This register deduplicates, merges, and prioritizes the eight participating perspectives' research requests into a single commissioned set. The Moderator executes it with WebSearch and WebFetch in the next sequential units, one or two items per iteration, writing each cited result to `research/r00-<topic>.md`. Items that cannot be retrieved with the available tools are flagged `NEEDS EXTERNAL RETRIEVAL` and carried to the DISSENT gate before Round 01 opens.

**Decision rule on retrieval method.** Every item is attempted by web retrieval first. Items marked **External-likely** depend on non-public artifacts (a private data room, paid patent-claim databases, or proprietary deal terms); for these, web retrieval captures the public proxy and the residual gap is flagged `NEEDS EXTERNAL RETRIEVAL` with the specific missing artifact named.

**Priority tiers.**
- **T1 Critical** — bears directly on whether the thesis survives; absence blocks Round 01.
- **T2 High** — materially shapes a SWOT quadrant or the strategy recommendation.
- **T3 Supporting** — sizes, contextualizes, or stress-tests a claim already in view.
